We do not exist in the majority of these;

in some you exist,

and not I;

in others I,

and not you;

in others,

both of us.

In the present one,

which a favourable fate has granted me,

you have arrived at my house;

in another,

while crossing the garden,

you found me dead;

in still another,

I utter these same words,

but I am a mistake, a ghost.
